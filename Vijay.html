Vijay

Referred to as "Thalapathy" (Commander) by his fans, Vijay has appeared in 69 films over a career spanning more than three decades. He debuted as a child actor in 1984 and achieved his first lead role in 1992. He is known for a mix of commercial blockbusters and critically successful films, often portraying action-oriented or socially conscious characters. He has announced that his upcoming film, titled Jana Nayagan (The People's Hero), will be his final film as a lead actor before fully transitioning to politics
